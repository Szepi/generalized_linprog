This paper proposes a generalized reduced linear program and developed
a corresponding analytical machinery so that the error can be proven to
be bounded.Some comments about this paper are as follows.

1. From the very beggining, it is unclear that what is the motivation
of this research and what does this research can be applied to. A
related management or control motivation example is needed to enhance
the motivation.

2.No recent related works are cited and discussed in this paper.Thus,
it is quite difficult to identify what the the innovation and
improvement of this paper comparing with existing research.

3.Some short explainations of the formulas can be helpful in
understanding this paper.
